Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Fweb?
Fweb is a fast, private, and user-friendly search engine built mainly for African users, focusing on locally relevant content while remaining accessible globally.
2. Is Fweb free to use?
Yes! Fweb is completely free for everyone and does not require any subscription or payment to search the web.
3. Does Fweb track my searches?
No. Fweb respects your privacy and does not track your searches or sell your data to advertisers.
4. Can anyone use Fweb outside Africa?
Absolutely. While Fweb prioritizes content relevant to Africa, anyone worldwide can use it seamlessly.
5. How can I contact support?
You can reach our support team through the Help page or the Contact page for any questions or issues.
