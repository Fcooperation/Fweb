Getting Started

To perform a search, type your query in the search bar and click the Search button. This will take you to the search page with results relevant to your query.

Using Search Effectively

Refining searches means researching further or narrowing down your query to get more specific results. Using precise keywords, like "best Nigerian recipes 2025," will help you find exactly what you need.

Understanding Search Results

Each result shows the title, a short description, and the source URL. Click any result to visit the page. Focus on results that are most relevant to your query.

Browser & Privacy Settings

Fweb keeps your searches private. Enhance security by not sharing passwords or secret codes. Your data is never sold or tracked.

Customizing Your Experience

You can change dark mode or language preference in the Settings page. Adjust the interface to suit your visual comfort and preferred language.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

If searches fail, the issue is usually a network problem or an error from Fweb's server. Try refreshing the page or checking your connection.

Tips & Tricks

Use specific keywords for faster results, focus on local content for Africa-related searches, and explore the site for news, culture, and education. Fweb makes research simple and safe.
