Introduction

At Fweb, your privacy is of utmost importance. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect your personal data while you use our search engine and related services. We are committed to transparency, security, and compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

Information We Collect

Fweb is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless voluntarily provided. The types of information that may be collected include:

  • Search queries and interactions with our platform for improving relevance and performance.
  • Technical information, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type, to ensure proper functionality.
  • Cookies and local storage data for user interface preferences, session continuity, and settings like dark mode and language preference.

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to enhance your experience on Fweb. This includes:

  • Delivering more relevant search results and improving search accuracy.
  • Maintaining a secure and private browsing environment.
  • Personalizing settings and preferences such as language and display mode.
  • Monitoring and optimizing site performance to ensure smooth functionality.

Security Measures

Fweb implements strong security protocols to protect your data. This includes:

  • Secure browsing via HTTPS encryption.
  • Strict access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Regular audits and monitoring to detect potential vulnerabilities.
  • Not storing passwords, payment information, or any sensitive personal codes unless explicitly provided by the user for account-related services.

Cookies and Tracking

Fweb uses minimal cookies strictly for functional purposes:

  • Remembering user interface preferences, such as dark mode or language settings.
  • Session continuity to maintain seamless search experience.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising, third-party tracking, or data selling.

Third-Party Services

Fweb may integrate third-party services for analytics or performance. We ensure that any such services comply with our strict privacy standards and do not expose personal data for commercial purposes.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review any information you have voluntarily provided.
  • Request deletion of personal data where applicable.
  • Opt-out of certain non-essential functional cookies.
  • Contact us for clarification or concerns regarding your data privacy.

Children’s Privacy

Fweb is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. Parents or guardians should supervise usage and contact us if they believe data from a child has been inadvertently collected.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or regulatory requirements. Users will be notified through the website or via email (if subscribed) about major updates. Continued use of Fweb constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
