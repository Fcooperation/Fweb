Top Search Categories

Education & Exams

Every year, students search for results, study materials, and exam tips. In 2025, queries about WAEC, NECO, JAMB, and scholarship opportunities remain at the top, reflecting the importance of education in Nigeria’s growth.

Technology & Gadgets

With smartphones and tech adoption soaring, Nigerians are searching for the latest gadgets, mobile apps, and tutorials. Keywords like “best Android apps 2025” or “cheap laptops in Nigeria” are increasingly popular.

Entertainment & Music

Music, movies, and trending celebrities dominate searches. Afrobeat, Nollywood films, and online streaming platforms are central to what users look for online.

Business & Money Making

Entrepreneurs and side-hustlers seek tips, investment ideas, and ways to make money online. Searches often include “how to make money online in Nigeria,” “crypto investment tips,” and “freelancing platforms.”

Health & Lifestyle

Queries about fitness, mental health, and wellness are growing. People want safe and reliable information on healthy living, diets, and lifestyle choices.
